## ğŸ‘¥ For Victims: Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Create Your SOS Post

1. Click **"Create SOS Post"** on the home page
2. Fill in the form with your details:
   - **Name:** Your full name
   - **Phone:** Your contact number
   - **Preferred Contact:** Choose how you want to be contacted
   - **Location:** Automatically detected (you'll see a green checkmark)
   - **Duration of Abuse:** Select from dropdown
   - **Frequency:** How often it happens
   - **Current Situation:** Describe what's happening
   - **Culprit Description:** Describe the person(s) involved

3. Click **"Generate SOS Post"**
4. Wait while AI processes your information (takes ~2 seconds)

### Step 2: Preview and Share Your Post

After submission, you'll see:
- An innocent-looking social media post (like a normal status update)
- A beautiful image (coffee, sunrise, or flowers)
- A green "âœ“ SOS Encoded" badge showing your message is hidden in the image
- Your post will look normal to others, but contains encoded help signals

**Share your post:**
- Click any of the share buttons (Telegram, Twitter, Instagram, Slack)
- You'll see a "Post Shared Successfully" message
- Your case is automatically saved

### Step 3: Track Your Case

Click **"View Your Dashboard"** to see:
- Your contact information
- Frequency and nature of violence
- Current status (Open/In Progress/Closed)
- A map showing your location
- Timeline of your case

### Step 4: Get Support

**Personal Care Assistant:**
- Click the purple chat icon or "Talk to Support Assistant"
- Ask for emotional support, encouragement, or motivation
- The AI will respond with compassionate, supportive messages
- Available 24/7

**Legal Advisor:**
- Click the blue scale icon or "Consult Legal Advisor"
- Ask questions about your legal rights
- Get information about domestic violence laws
- Learn about custody, property rights, and legal protection
- Free legal aid information provided

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: How does the SOS encoding work?**
A: The AI generates an innocent-looking social media post. In a real system, steganography would hide your actual message in the image. This demo simulates that process.

**Q: Can others see my real message?**
A: In this demo, the post looks innocent to everyone. Only authorities with decoding tools could extract the hidden SOS (in a real implementation).

**Q: How do I update my case?**
A: View your dashboard to see current status. Authorities can update status from their end.

**Q: Is the AI real?**
A: The responses are simulated for this demo. A production system would use actual AI models.

**Q: Can I create multiple cases?**
A: Yes! Click "Create SOS Post" again to submit a new case.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Clear your browser's local storage to delete all cases.
